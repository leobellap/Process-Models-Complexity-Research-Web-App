3. Remote patient monitoring

This dataset describes the process that occurs during remote blood pressure monitoring of patients at home (e.g., doctor consultation, medication administration, etc.).

The model was formed based on data obtained during remote monitoring of a group of patients with arterial hypertension.

As part of this process, home blood pressure measurements are transmitted to the health care provider, then to the operator, and finally to the attending physician.
