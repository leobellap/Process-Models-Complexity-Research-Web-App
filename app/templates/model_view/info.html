Process model evaluation

The process models in this survey describe various medical procedures (patient treatment pipeline, hospital operations, etc.). These models are often used in medical establishments as frameworks for treatment & healthcare guidelines. By analysing how human experts understand and interpret these process models, we aim to extract the level of complexity at which the model is considered balanced (i.e. neither oversimplified nor extremely complex).

The figure below describes process modelling. Where:

  1. The Event Log is a process represented in textual format with information such as Time, Date, Activity Occurring & Unique ID.
  2. Process Mining is the methodology of transforming an Event log by Mining it with an Algorithm to create a Process Model.
  3. The Process Model is a visual representation of the sequence of activities occurring in the event log.
