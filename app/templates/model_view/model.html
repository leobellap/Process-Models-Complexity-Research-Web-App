About

This process model describes events performed in a hospital to treat Sepsis.

Sepsis is a life-threatening reaction to an infection. It happens when a persons immune system overreacts to an infection and starts to damage the body's own tissues and organs.

The data here, was extracted from the ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system of the hospital.
